Utah, United States of America


Utah is a state in the Western United States. It is known for it's mountain ranges and deserts. It is the 34th most populated state in the US. It's capital is Salt Lake City. In 2002 the winter Olympics were hosted in that city. It is famous for being the home of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The first members of that religion moved there in 1847.
