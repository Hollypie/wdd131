faithful PAGES WEBSITE PLAN

Site Purpose:

This website is a place to publish my blog about Scripture Journaling. It features my faithfilled art and perhaps a place to sell stickers and bookmarks. I has a form for followers to get email notifications for updates and sales and deals. And maybe an area for reviews of common journaling supplies. Also a form for indy companies to have their products featured and reviewed. Or perhaps a listing of clubs that meet for scripture journaling gatherings

Scenarios:

What are the best beginner supplies for scripture jouraling? What clubs are avaliable locally that meet regularly to scripture journal?
